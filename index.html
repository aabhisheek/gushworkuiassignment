Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion Process

State-of-the-art extruders melt and form the PE compound into a continuous pipe profile at controlled temperatures and pressure.

  • Controlled melt temperature
  • Consistent wall thickness control

Controlled Cooling

The extruded pipe passes through calibrated water cooling troughs to achieve optimum molecular structure and dimensional stability.

  • Calibrated water troughs
  • Uniform quenching profile

Precise Sizing & Calibration

Vacuum sizing ensures precise outer diameter meets nominal dimensions per IS 4984 and ISO 4427 standards throughout the full run.

  • ±0.1mm dimensional tolerance
  • Real-time OD measurement

Rigorous Quality Control

Every batch undergoes hydrostatic pressure testing, dimensional inspection, and MFR verification before release.

  • Hydrostatic burst testing
  • Third-party NABL lab certification

Marking & Identification

Pipes are indelibly marked every metre with product details: size, SDR, pressure rating, material grade, and production batch code.

  • Indelible ink marking
  • Batch traceability codes

Precision Cutting

Automated planetary saw cutting ensures burr-free, square ends at specified lengths with tight tolerance.

  • Automated saw cutting
  • Custom cut lengths available

Packaging & Dispatch

Pipes are coiled, strapped, and palletised per customer requirements, then dispatched with full test certificate documentation.

  • Protective packaging
  • Test certificates included
